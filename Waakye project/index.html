My Self

I am Kennesa Arhin, a girl born 18 years ago. I hail from Agonyakrom in the Central Region but i reside at Effiakuma. I am tall and a bit plump. I have small but , pointed nose and cute lips on my broad face. I am a SHS graduate who completed Archbishop Porter Girls Secondary School. I am a catholic and I therefore attend Christ the King Catholic Church which is located at Effiakuma. I have two siblings, we all being the children of Mr. and Mrs Arhin. I am a hardworking girl who loves singing, reading and cooking. I really love myself because I am simple and nice.
